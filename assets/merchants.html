FAQs
Have a question? A source of useful information about our products and services.
How do I accept cryptocurrencies?
It’s really easy to integrate B2BinPay crypto payment gateway into your current operations or launch it as a new service. Forward us your request, proceed with the integration and you’re ready to offer your customers Bitcoin(BTC), Ether(ETH), Ripple (XRP), Bitcoin Cash (BCH), Litecoin(LTC) and a whole range more as a payment method!
What cryptocurrencies are on offer?
B2BinPay can handle all your cryptocurrency processing needs with cryptos including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Bitcoin Cash, Monero, Binance Coin, Stellar, Zcash, Cardano, EOS, Ripple, Nem, Doge, TRON, NEM. A total of 6 stablecoins and 888+ tokens are available for your convenience.
Are StableCoins and Tokens supported?
Yes, Tether, TrueUSD, USD Coin, Binance USD, Gemini, Paxos and more than 888 NEO, NEM, ETH and Omni-based tokens are all supported.
Is a test account available?
Integration first takes place in a staging environment for the purpose of client testing. Production deployment only takes place after this process is approved so as to limit the risk of bugs during the production stage. This procedure ensures that you will have the most stable product on the market!
How do I integrate a crypto payment gateway?
Integrating cryptocurrency payments into your existing system couldn’t be easier. Integration is made via simple API in matter of minutes
Is B2BinPay secure?
Yes, of course! We take security very seriously and never ever compromise. As specialists in our field, we always offer our clients a safe, secure and reliable environment. Our API endpoints are encrypted through SSL so user traffic is secured when requesting data.
Are automatic withdrawals supported?
Yes, automatic withdrawals are possible via secure API directly to your own or your clients’ wallet.
Do you have any restrictions for my country?
B2BinPay does not offer services to residents of USA, Afghanistan, American Samoa, the Bahamas, Botswana, Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Ethiopia, Ghana, Guam, Iran, Iraq, Libya, Nigeria, Pakistan, Panama, Puerto Rico, Samoa, Saudi Arabia, Sri Lanka, Syria, Trinidad and Tobago, Tunisia, US Virgin Islands and Yemen.
Is B2BinPay regulated?
Yes, we are regulated by the Estonian Government.
Am I able to accept payments using Visa/Master with your system?
No, we only work with crypto. In order to avoid risks associated with volatility, you can exchange your crypto into fiat and withdraw directly to your SEPA or SWIFT bank account.
Is it possible to open a wallet as an individual?
Yes, you can open a Crypto/Crypto merchant account quickly and easily.
Where can I check conversion rates?
