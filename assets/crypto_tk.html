A Crypto Broker is a new business model that combines the best practices from both worlds: an innovative cryptocurrency trading exchange and a classic brokerage. Customer Evolution Map
Each customer goes through several roles during his life-time and use different products. Clients usually start with simple trading account and then follow it with social trading or PAMM accounts.
Stage
Client’s action
Broker’s action
Opportunities

Lead

Sees an advert and gets interested in the financial markets. This is a good opportunity to make some money from savings, become rich and look smart in the eyes of friends.

Initiate retargeting advertising and follow-up leads via phone and emails

Not too much can be done during this step. The main purpose is to get the client registered.

Use instruments that increase conversion and simplify the first step for getting clients’ contacts.

Registered User

Client goes though registration process and verifies his phone number and email.

Guide a client through the registration process and make the next steps clear so he knows what account to open and how to use it.

Offer him a free giveaway after registration such as a trading course or welcome bonus.

Client

Has passed KYC, opens a new account and makes a first deposit. He already knows what his next step is i.e. to be an investor or trader as this was his initial motivation for opening an account and making a deposit.

Chat to, and better still, call a registered user if he failed to complete KYC or the deposit process. He is now just a few steps ahead of making a deposit so don’t lose him.

Send auto-emails for all registered, but not active clients. Hire 1or 2 Sales managers to work with such clients.

Active Trader

The client has downloaded the trading termninal and opens positions. He is trying to develop a trading strategy and follow it.

Communicate with a client thought via email, write blog posts, develop new offers for active clients such as free VPS, reward programms for big commissions or contests.

All traders have 2 paths: Become a PRO or start investing. The broker therefore needs to monitor the activity of each client. If he earns profits for 2 months in a row, he can be offered the chance to become a money manager or signal provider and earn more on fees. If he is unable to make profits, he can become an investor or follow some trading courses.

Consider “Active Trader” stage not as a goal but as a temporary state of a client’s activity and help him to evolve by offering more suitable products via content marketing or direct mail and phone calls.

Active Investor

He goes to the leaderboard and checks the statistics of traders. If he likes it, he opens an account and follows one or several money managers.

Once he starts following them, he can simply monitor the performance. This role requires no actions from his side if an investor set a risk limit.

Each client can be trader and investor at the same time. This situation can last for as long as it is comfortable. The broker should promote the opportunity to become an investor via blogs, youtube videos and email to those clients who are unable to earn by themselves and all registered users who didn’t open a trading account.

Convert an upset trader who is unable to make any profit to a happy, active investor. Just call or email him.

Frequently undertake reviews of signal providers by email and blogs so clients are made aware of the existence of good traders.

Professional Trader

He knows how to make money on the market. He is now ready to become a signal provider and multiply his returns with investors money so he opens a master account and starts trading on it.

The best thing a broker can offer this clients beyond trading conditions is to become a money manager and convert his previous trading history into a nice chart for a leaderboard of strategies.

Being a signal provider is more valuable than just personal success and multiplying your own savings.

Offer all your customers who can trade well the chance to become a signal provider. This will not only give you a potential advantage in investment products but will also make your PRO traders more loyal to your company which helped them make a career in trading and earn real money.

Give them a personal account manager for exceptional support of your most valuable clients.

Inactive

Sometimes clients stop trading or investing. Some people never become an active trader / investor. They just register and even make a deposit but never go on to make a trade. Each has his own history, but a broker should still work with them and convert them into active clients. It is much cheaper than attempting to attract new customers.

Calls and Emails Email him once a month or during big market movements and call a client to offer a product he has never used. You can also inform him what has changed since his last authorization in TR. Of course, you should continue to publish new content on all social media channels.

Create trading/investment courses or a webinar series.

Reactivate a client by offering new products if he hasn’t yet used them before as an alternative to his previous approach e.g. use EA instead of personal trading, or become a follower instead of using EAs or trading on his own. Maybe he could subscribe to MQL signals?

Call him if possible, otherwise, email him. Client reactivation is the cheapest way to grow your client base.
