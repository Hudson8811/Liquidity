Business Models
Our core products comprise of MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5 White Label, B2Trader (Matching Engine), B2Margin (Margin Exchange) and B2Core (Trader's Room). A new generation of professional products that helps brokers and exchanges.
Our liquidity pool combines FOREX, Crypto Derivatives/CFD, Metals, Indices, Energy, Equities CFD, ETFs CFD. We have the deepest and most diverse liquidity pool available in the industry to match every requirement.
Our FOREX and Crypto turnkey solutions are ready-to-use. An all-in-one package of services and software solutions that helps entrepreneurs launch their new brokerage in minimal time and with lower financial outlay.
Boost your revenue-potential with our Money Management solutions and offer Copy Trading, MAM and PAMM via our Investment Platform. Designed to help you attract the best traders and money managers and assist beginners with copying successful positions from the professionals.
Accept Cryptocurrency payments and offer a service to your customers quickly and easily. Our Crypto Processing range of solutions include Merchant and Enterprise Wallets solutions for every type of business need.
Our Turnkey Crypto Spot Exchange allows you to launch your own Cryptocurrency Excahnge in 1 month with 24/7 support, comprehensive training and a vast range of other features. Benefit from a proven business model.
Our Software Solutions are developed so our clients reap the beneftis of seamless procedures and a smooth operation of all applications.
We provide a range of Corporate Services associated with legal and IT support. As experts in our field, we have accumulated a minefield of knowledge so that you can swiftly get started with your brokerage operations.
We develop Business Models for our clients to help with optimisation of their business. We have the know how to implement appropriate techniques that are the perfect fit for your business and help you achieve your goals.
